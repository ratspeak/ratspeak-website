Why Ratspeak

Built without compromise.

Not just another encrypted messenger. An entirely different network, where privacy, identity, and self-sovereignty are built into the protocol itself.

No Account

Your identity is a cryptographic key — generated by you, owned by nobody else. No email, no phone number, no registration.

Privacy First

Messages, packets, routing — encrypted at every layer. Even the network itself can't see what you're sending, or to who.

Transport Agnostic

WiFi, LoRa, 5G, Bluetooth, anything. If it moves data, it works, and they all use the same private transport protocol.

Built Resilient

No servers. No database. No single point of control. The mesh routes around failures on its own.

Multi-Platform

Desktop, mobile, MCU, SBC. One protocol shared across all form factors.

Open Source

Every line of code is public. Read it, audit it, fork it. Trust through transparency.

Getting Started

Four steps. That's it.

Download, launch, connect, talk. No setup wizards, no config files, no waiting.

1. Download

Grab the app or flash your device. A couple clicks, that's it.

2. Create Identity

Initial setup generates a cryptographic identity that always belongs to just you.

3. Connect

Pick your transport method, and the mesh handles the rest.

4. Start Talking

Send messages, files, play games, you're in — welcome to a better system.
