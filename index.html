How It Works

1

Create an Identity

Your device generates its own cryptographically secure keys. No account. No signup.

2

Connect to Networks

Connect to a device, network, or create one to begin finding other available peers.

3

Route Formation

Nodes learn who can reach who and form efficient routing on their own. No servers. No central list.

4

Always Encrypted

Messages are end-to-end encrypted prior to leaving your device. The network knows data is moving, but not where, or who it's from.

5

Send Over Anything

Data travels through any available link - Radio, WiFi, TCP, BLE, etc., until they reach the desired recipient.
